
# RAD Morocco - Enhanced Travel Website with AI Assistant

## Overview
A modern, animated recreation of the RAD Morocco travel website with improved design, smooth animations, and an AI-powered travel assistant to help customers plan their perfect Moroccan journey.

---

- **Animated RAD logo** with the distinctive blue "R" branding
- **Scroll-triggered animations** for content sections
- **Quick navigation** to all major sections
- **Featured tour packages** carousel with hover effects
- **"Let us know what you're looking for"** call-to-action leading to AI assistant

## 2. About Us Page

## 8. AI Travel Assistant (Floating Chat)

### Floating Chat Widget
- **Elegant chat button** in corner of every page
- **Opens into a chat interface** with RAD Morocco branding
- **Available 24/7** to help customers

### AI Capabilities
The assistant will be able to help with:

1. **Trip Planning Advice**
   - Suggest destinations based on interests
   - Recommend best times to visit Morocco
   - Create sample itineraries
   - Explain what each city/region offers

2. **Budget Estimation**
   - Provide rough cost estimates based on:
     - Duration of stay
     - Accommodation type (hotels, riads, glamping)
     - Activities selected
     - Group size
   - Explain what's included in packages

3. **FAQs & Travel Info**
   - Visa requirements for different countries
   - Weather information by season
   - What to pack
   - Cultural tips and etiquette
   - Currency and payment info
   - Safety information

4. **Guide Through Booking**
   - Walk customers through the reservation form
   - Explain the booking process
   - Answer questions about tours
